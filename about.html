doe & fawn

doe & fawn is the baby of a group of girls in their 20s who were entering the age where entering the age where they were starting to be invited to a lot of baby showers. Wanting a unique gift that would stand out from the crowd but wanting to make sure their gift was useful and appropriate- we spent hours searching the internet but ended up not knowing what to buy.

As we oogled and awed at all the products out there- we realized that we wanted to give more people’s friends and family the option to express their love and excitement. We hit the streets to talk to new mommies and trial run toys with one of the team’s 6 month old nephew and are proud to introduce out specially curated bundles to fawn over!
